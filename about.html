About Resizer-img

Learn more about our image resizing service.

Our Story

Resizer-img was created with a simple goal in mind: to provide a fast, secure, and easy-to-use tool for image resizing. We understand the importance of properly sized images for websites, social media, and various other purposes.

Our tool is designed to be straightforward and efficient. Unlike other image processing services, we prioritize your privacy by ensuring all image processing happens right in your browser. Your images never leave your device, giving you complete control and security.

Our Mission

We aim to simplify image resizing for everyone, from professional designers to casual users. Our commitment is to provide a reliable, user-friendly tool that helps you resize images quickly without sacrificing quality.

Image Processing

How It Works

Resizer-img uses modern web technologies to process your images:

  • Client-side Processing: All image manipulation happens in your browser, ensuring privacy and speed.
  • HTML5 Canvas: We use the HTML5 Canvas API to resize your images with high precision.
  • No Server Uploads: Your images are never uploaded to any server, making the process secure and fast.

The Team

Resizer-img is maintained by a small team of passionate developers led by Rishav Srivastava. We are committed to continuously improving our tool and adding new features based on user feedback.
