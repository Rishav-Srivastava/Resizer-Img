Frequently Asked Questions

Is my image data secure when using Resizer-img?

Yes! All image processing happens directly in your browser. Your images are never uploaded to our servers.

What image formats do you support?

Our tool supports JPG, PNG, GIF, and WebP formats.

Is there a size limit for image uploads?

The size limit depends on your browser's memory capabilities. Generally, images up to 10-20MB should work fine.

Do you have a mobile app?

Currently, we only offer a web application, but it's fully responsive and works great on mobile devices.
